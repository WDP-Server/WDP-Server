## Earning Strategy

### Best Skills for Coins

1. **Foraging** - Fast XP, easy to level
2. **Mining** - Good XP, useful resources
3. **Farming** - Passive XP while AFK

### Quick Tips

- Use abilities when available
- AFK overnight where possible
- Focus one skill at a time
- Complete daily quests alongside

## Progress Integration

Each level up:
- Adds coins to balance
- Updates WDP-Progress score
- Registers in achievements
