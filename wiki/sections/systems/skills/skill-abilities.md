# Skill Abilities

**Active abilities for each skill**

## Overview

Skills have active abilities that enhance gameplay. Unlocked at certain levels.

## Mining Abilities

### Mining Drill
- **Unlock:** Level 5
- **Effect:** 2x mining speed
- **Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 30 seconds

### Super Breaker
- **Unlock:** Level 15
- **Effect:** 3x mining speed
- **Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 45 seconds

## Foraging Abilities

### Tree Feller
- **Unlock:** Level 5
- **Effect:** Chop entire tree at once
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cooldown:** 20 seconds

### Natures Grace
- **Unlock:** Level 15
- **Effect:** 2x chopping speed
- **Duration:** 10 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 40 seconds

## Farming Abilities

### Green Thumb
- **Unlock:** Level 5
- **Effect:** Harvest radius 3x3
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cooldown:** 15 seconds

### Farmer's Fortune
- **Unlock:** Level 20
- **Effect:** Double drops
- **Duration:** 10 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 60 seconds

## Agility Abilities

### Speed Boost
- **Unlock:** Level 3
- **Effect:** +50% movement speed
- **Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 45 seconds

### Double Jump
- **Unlock:** Level 10
- **Effect:** Jump again in air
- **Duration:** Instant
- **Cooldown:** 5 seconds

## Using Abilities

```
Click ability icon in skills menu
or
Use bound key (if configured)
```

## Ability Tips

1. **Pair with tools** - Efficiency enchantments stack
2. **Save for AFK** - Max efficiency while away
3. **Chain abilities** - Some abilities chain well
4. **Check cooldowns** - Don't waste on small tasks
